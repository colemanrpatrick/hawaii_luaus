Aha'aina Luau
"A great night of entertainment"

We had a great night. Learning some Hula moves and making traditional Lei bracelets plus other cultural activities before the show. ...The food was traditional and very tasty as were the cocktails. A beautiful sunset in the background was an added bonus as was the fireworks from the Hilton later in the night. Strongly recommend doing this on your visit.
